Founder & CEO

Meet Emily.

Emily Nelson is a former broadcast journalist and Fortune 100 top sales executive with 15+ years of experience crafting proven sales, media and storytelling strategies that drive growth. She's worked with hundreds of C-level executives as a trusted advisor and is passionate about consistently exceeding client expectations with laser focused results.

Emily graduated with an MBA from Northwestern University's Kellogg School of Management and an MSJ from Northwestern's Medill School of Journalism. She received her BS in Marketing from The Ohio State University. Emily lives in Los Angeles and is a self-proclaimed foodie and travel enthusiast. She's also active in the community and volunteers as a Court-Appointed Special Advocate (CASA) serving foster youth and is a guest speaker at the USC Marshall School of Business.
